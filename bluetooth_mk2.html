Bluetooth LE MK2 PCB

This prototype board was designed for Bluetooth LE connectivity. It has a custom developed PCB antenna operating at 2.4 GHz. A matching circuit is also designed for the feeder of the antenna. The antenna was designed using Microwave Office software by National Instruments. After EM optimisations on Microwave Office the antenna was designed in the Altium designer

Top layer of the Bluetooth LE circuit using Nordic Semiconductors SoC
